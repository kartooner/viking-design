# Viking Design Framework: The Creative Skill Chain

This document defines the specialized roles and the collaboration sequence within the Viking methodology.

## The Skill Chain

### 1. Odin (Creative Director)
- **Entry Point**: Receives the initial brief.
- **Mandate**: Leads the **5 Gates** review. Sets the creative mandate and business goals.
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-director.md`

### 2. Mimir (UX Researcher)
- **Role**: Discovery and validation.
- **Mandate**: Runs the **5 Gates** fit assessment. Audits mental models and user vocabulary.
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-researcher.md`

### 3. Tyr (Creative Strategist)
- **Role**: Problem framing and opportunity sizing.
- **Mandate**: Translates business goals into user-centric opportunities. Defines the "User Hats."
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-strategist.md`

### 4. Heimdall (Systems Architect)
- **Role**: Logic mapping (Things, States, Rules).
- **Mandate**: Defines the **AI Placement** and **HITL** moments. Leads the **Architect Pairing**.
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-architect.md`

### 5. Loki (Flow Designer)
- **Role**: Interaction design and wireflows.
- **Mandate**: Designs ephemeral, generative UI recipes based on system mapping.
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-flow-designer.md`

### 6. Bragi (Content Designer)
- **Role**: Language and voice.
- **Mandate**: Crafts "Beautiful UX Copy" that adheres to the Disney-ish warmth while maintaining Viking resilience. Uses the **Sandwich Pattern**.
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-content-designer.md`

### 7. Thor (Handoff Specialist)
- **Role**: Technical documentation.
- **Mandate**: Delivers **Component Contracts** and **Normative Tokens** to Engineering.
- **Reference**: `agents/viking-handoff-specialist.md`

---

## Shared Principles
- **Systems First**: No role designs screens in isolation. All work flows from the object model.
- **Compact-First**: Every role prioritizes density and utility.
- **Glassbox Default**: Transparency is the standard for high-stakes AI moments.
- **Dual-Track**: Discovery and Delivery run in parallel pods.
